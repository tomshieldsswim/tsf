Why Events

More Than Fundraising

Community events do something grants and donations alone can't: they create belonging. When a team swims together to fund something that matters, that's a workshop before the workshop even happens.

🌊

Build Community

Swim events bring athletes, families, coaches, and supporters together around a shared purpose — reducing isolation, one of the biggest risk factors for youth mental health struggles.

💰

Fund the Mission

Every dollar raised goes directly toward delivering free or subsidized mental health education workshops to youth athletes at schools, clubs, and sports organizations.

📣

Start the Conversation

Swim-a-thons create natural moments to talk about why we're here — putting mental health on the agenda in spaces where it's rarely discussed.
