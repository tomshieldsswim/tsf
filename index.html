The Mission

The Tom Shields Foundation funds mental health appointments for youth athletes and installs evidence-based mental health training at swim clubs, powered by the swim community.

"The system isn't built to find the kid who's learned to hide it. We give the people in the room the tools to see it."
, Tom Shields, Founder
